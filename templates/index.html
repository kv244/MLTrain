How to Play

  • Two players take turns dropping coloured pieces into a 7×6 grid.
  • Pieces fall to the lowest empty slot in the chosen column.
  • First to connect 4 in a line — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal — wins.
  • If all 42 squares fill up with no winner, the game is a draw.
  • Tip: raise Think Intensity for a stronger (but slower) AI.
